About 22UponTwo

We started off as two twenty-two year olds who wanted to do more than just market products.

We strongly believed, and still believe in the power young local creatives have to exploit the digital landscape and create transformative solutions for businesses. We have now welcomed many young people into our team to help brands grow through our portfolio of products.

Four Tailored
Solutions

Web Development

Our team of web developers are ready to listen to your requirement and offer a unique solution for your business/event website. We have a number of different packages (Customized / Wordpress) that you can choose from.

Content Creation

Our writers, designers and illustrators create content that is of high quality with a unique identity. Whether you simply want to tweak the content that you already have or create completely new material, we are here to help.

Social Media Marketing

It is crucial to make sound decisions when investing on marketing your products on social media. Our experience and culture of constant learning gives us the tools to help brands make the most of their social media presence.

Identity Creation

If you are just starting your brand and need a complete set of solutions from logo design, web/ social media implementations, business card/ letterhead/ envelope design, we are catered to offer you our services at a reasonable price.
